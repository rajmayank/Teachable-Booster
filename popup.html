Teachable Booster

Now view the overall section time and also the lecture running time with respect to the section.
The first timestamp is at the playback speed that you are using for your course and the second one is the original timestamp i.e. at playback speed 1x.

Example: At 2x playback speed, a 5 min lecture would have the following info : [2:50 | 5:00]
Open this plugin from any Teachable Course page.
